IT Onboarding Checklist
New Employee IT Setup Guide
Before Day 1
* IT will provision your laptop and email account 3 business days before your start date.
* You will receive a welcome email with your employee ID and temporary password.
Day 1: Account Setup
* Log in to your laptop using the temporary password provided in your welcome email.
* You will be prompted to create a new password. Passwords must be at least 12 characters, include one number, and one special character.
* Set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) using the Okta Verify app on your phone.
* Log in to your company email account (Outlook) and set up your email signature.
Day 1: Required Software Installation
* Slack (company communication) — sign in with your company email.
* Zoom (video conferencing) — sign in via Single Sign-On (SSO).
* VPN client — required for remote access to internal systems. Contact IT Help Desk for the install link if not pre-installed.
* Password manager (1Password) — you will receive an invite link via email.
Week 1: System Access
* Submit an access request form for any department-specific software (e.g., Salesforce, Workday, Jira) through the IT Service Portal.
* Access approval typically takes 1–2 business days depending on your manager's sign-off.
* Confirm access to shared drives relevant to your team by checking with your manager.
Hardware
* Laptop, charger, and headset are provided on Day 1.
* Monitor and keyboard/mouse can be requested through the IT Service Portal if working from the office.
* Report any hardware issues to the IT Help Desk at ithelpdesk@company.com or extension 4357.
Security Policies
* Do not share your password with anyone, including IT staff.
* Lock your screen (Windows key + L) whenever you step away from your desk.
* Report suspicious emails using the "Report Phishing" button in Outlook.
* All company data must be stored on approved cloud storage (OneDrive), not local drives or personal devices.
Support
* IT Help Desk hours: Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–6:00 PM.
* Submit tickets through the IT Service Portal for non-urgent issues.
* Call extension 4357 for urgent, blocking issues (e.g., cannot log in, VPN not working).
