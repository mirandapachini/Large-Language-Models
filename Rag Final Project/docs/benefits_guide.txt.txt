# Benefits Enrollment Guide

**New Hire Benefits Guide**

## Enrollment Deadline
- New hires have **30 days from their start date** to enroll in benefits through the Workday Benefits portal.
- If you do not enroll within 30 days, you will automatically be assigned the default plan (Basic PPO, employee-only coverage) and must wait until the next Open Enrollment period (November) to make changes, unless you experience a qualifying life event.

## Health Insurance Plans
- **Basic PPO**: Lower monthly premium, higher deductible ($2,500 individual). Company covers 70% of premium cost.
- **Premium PPO**: Higher monthly premium, lower deductible ($500 individual). Company covers 70% of premium cost.
- **HDHP + HSA**: High-deductible plan paired with a Health Savings Account. Company contributes $750/year to your HSA if you enroll in this plan.
- Dependent coverage (spouse/domestic partner and children) is available on all three plans at an additional employee cost.

## Dental and Vision
- Dental coverage is optional and costs $18/month for employee-only coverage, $45/month for family coverage.
- Vision coverage is optional and costs $8/month for employee-only coverage, $20/month for family coverage.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan
- Eligible to enroll immediately upon hire.
- Company matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary, then 50% of the next 2% (effectively up to 5% match).
- Enrollment and contribution changes can be made anytime through the Fidelity NetBenefits portal, not just during Open Enrollment.
- Vesting schedule: employee contributions are always 100% vested; company match vests 20% per year, fully vested after 5 years.

## Life and Disability Insurance
- Basic life insurance (1x annual salary) is provided free of charge to all full-time employees.
- Supplemental life insurance can be purchased at additional cost, up to 5x annual salary, with proof of good health for amounts over $500,000.
- Short-term and long-term disability insurance are included at no cost for all full-time employees after 90 days of employment.

## Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)
- Healthcare FSA: contribute up to $3,200/year pre-tax for eligible medical expenses.
- Dependent Care FSA: contribute up to $5,000/year pre-tax for childcare or eldercare expenses.
- FSA elections must be renewed every year during Open Enrollment; funds do not automatically roll over except up to $640 for the Healthcare FSA.

## Questions
- Contact the Benefits Team at benefits@company.com or schedule a 1:1 benefits consultation through the HR Portal during your first 30 days.
